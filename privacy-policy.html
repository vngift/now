Introduction

At Vanilla Gift, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and transparently. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with Google Ads policies.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and contact details when you register or contact us.
  • Payment Information: Transaction history for balance checks and gift card purchases.
  • Browsing Data: Cookies, device type, browser type, and IP address for improving website performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To process gift card registration and check balances securely.
  • To improve customer service and provide support as requested.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure security.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers only when necessary to provide our services, as long as they adhere to confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (SSL), secure databases, and restricted access to sensitive data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we recommend caution when sharing personal details online.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, track performance, and serve personalized ads in line with Google Ads guidelines. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as per our cookie policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights or opt out of data tracking, please contact us at support@vanillagift.com.
